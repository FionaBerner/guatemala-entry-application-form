# 🏞️ Top Places to Visit in Guatemala

Guatemala is one of Central America’s most culturally rich and naturally diverse destinations. These highlights represent the country’s most iconic and rewarding places to explore.

**A kind reminder about the documents needed to visit Guatemala**: Do not forget that Guatemala has set a mandatory online travel permit for everyone visiting the country. It's called [Declaración jurada del viajero Guatemala](https://guatemalaentryform.com/). Once you have it approved on your names, you are ready to go.

## Antigua Guatemala
A UNESCO World Heritage city known for:
- Colonial architecture
- Colorful streets
- Volcano views
- Coffee farms and cultural tours

## Lake Atitlán
A volcanic lake surrounded by traditional Mayan villages. Popular activities include:
- Boat tours
- Hiking
- Kayaking
- Visiting artisan markets

## Tikal National Park
One of the most impressive archaeological sites in the Americas:
- Ancient Mayan pyramids
- Dense jungle wildlife
- Sunrise and sunset tours

## Semuc Champey
A natural limestone bridge with turquoise pools, ideal for:
- Swimming
- Cave exploration
- Nature photography

## Chichicastenango Market
One of the largest traditional markets in Central America:
- Handicrafts
- Textiles
- Ceremonial sites
- Local food
