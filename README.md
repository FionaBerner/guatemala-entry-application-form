# 🧭 Guatemala Entry Application Form — Your Guide to a Smooth Arrival

Foreign travelers visiting Guatemala must complete the [**Guatemala Entry Application Form**](https://guatemalaentryform.com/) before arrival. This online form helps immigration authorities verify traveler information in advance and ensures a faster, smoother entry at La Aurora International Airport and land borders.

## Why the Entry Application Form Matters
- It is a **mandatory** requirement for foreign visitors.
- It speeds up immigration processing.
- It reduces paperwork and waiting times.
- It ensures your travel information is pre‑verified before arrival.

## How the Form Works
- The form is completed online before your flight.
- Travelers typically fill it out **24 hours to 1 hour** before departure.
- After submission, you receive a confirmation that immigration officers may request to see.
- Keep the confirmation accessible on your phone or printed.
